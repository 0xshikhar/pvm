# PolkaBasket: The Social Cross-Chain DeFi Index Layer

### 🚀 Vision
PolkaBasket aims to become the definitive DeFi index layer of Polkadot, enabling users to access diversified cross-chain yield with a single click. Powered by **PolkaVM** and **XCM v5**, we unify the ecosystem's liquidity into a seamless, high-performance yield layer that abstracts all technical complexity.

### 🌟 The Social Winning Point: Cross-Chain Social Hub
We are evolving from a purely technical primitive into a **Cross-Chain Social Hub**. This is our unique differentiator:
- **Swipe-to-Invest:** A high-end, Tinder-style discovery engine where users can effortlessly explore and invest in different basket strategies with a single swipe.
- **User-Generated Strategies:** Empowering the community to create, name, and deploy their own custom asset baskets on the Hub.
- **Social Sharing & Invites:** Share your top-performing strategies with friends and invite them to joint-investing pools.
- **Basket Rewards:** Earn incentives and reputation based on the performance and popularity of the baskets you curate.

### 🛠️ Technical Stack
- **PolkaVM (RISC-V):** Our Rust-based allocation engine runs natively as a PolkaVM precompile for ultra-fast, safe financial rebalancing.
- **XCM v5:** Secure, SCALE-encoded cross-chain messaging for multi-hop asset deployment.
- **ERC-8004:** Standard-compliant cross-chain indexing for 1:1 asset backing.
- **Polkadot Hub:** Deployed on the Paseo Testnet (Chain ID: 420420417).

PolkaBasket doesn't just move capital; it moves communities, making cross-chain DeFi human, social, and accessible.
